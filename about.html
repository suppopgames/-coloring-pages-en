Our Story

It all started at home, with two amazing and imaginative children – Ari and Nir. Ari, the older one, loves action. He can spend hours drawing his favorite soccer players scoring a winning goal, or superheroes saving the world. Nir, the younger one, is an artist of colors and shapes. He loves everything on wheels, the characters from his favorite shows, and always adds a touch of red, his favorite color, to every creation.

As a father, I wanted to create a single place for them where their imagination could flourish without limits. A place with endless coloring pages, of all kinds and for all ages. That's how "Ari & Nir's Coloring World" was born.

But we wanted to add a little more magic. We thought to ourselves, "What if every drawing could tell a story?". And here, with the help of a little magical artificial intelligence technology, we turned that thought into reality. Next to each coloring page, you'll find a small button that can invent a personal and new story just for your drawing, turning the coloring experience into a complete adventure.

This website is a gift from us, a dad, Ari, and Nir, to all the children (and parents) everywhere. We hope you enjoy coloring, imagining, and creating your own stories.

With lots of love,
The Coloring World Team
