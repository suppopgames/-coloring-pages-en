Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 29, 2025

Welcome to "Ari & Nir's Coloring World" (the "Website"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

Information Collection

We collect two types of information:

  • Non-Personal Information: Like most websites, we collect information that your browser sends automatically, such as browser type, device type, and anonymous statistical data about website usage. This information helps us understand how to improve the site.
  • Personal Information: We only collect personal information when you voluntarily provide it to us. For example, if you contact us through a "Contact Us" form (to be established in the future). Participation in on-site polls is completely anonymous.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Improve and maintain our website and services.
  • Understand which content and coloring pages are most popular.
  • Respond to your inquiries and questions.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services to provide a better experience:

  • Gemini AI (Google): The "Create a Story" feature uses Google's Gemini artificial intelligence model. When you click this button, the request (e.g., "a story about a cute lion") is sent to Google's servers to generate the response. We do not send any personally identifiable information with the request.
  • Advertisements (Future): We may use advertising services like Google AdSense. These vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Intellectual Property and Fair Use

We invest significant effort in creating original and unique coloring pages.

  • The vast majority of the coloring pages on the site were created by us, often with the assistance of AI tools, and are our intellectual property.
  • All coloring pages on the site are intended for **personal, domestic, and educational use only**. You may not distribute, sell, or make any commercial use of the coloring pages without our express written permission.
  • If we use images or elements permitted for commercial use, we do so in accordance with their license terms. If you believe that any content on the site infringes your copyright, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy very seriously. The site is intended for a general audience but is child-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian discovers that their child has provided us with personal information without their consent, they should contact us immediately. We will delete such information from our servers as soon as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We recommend visiting this page periodically to stay informed.
